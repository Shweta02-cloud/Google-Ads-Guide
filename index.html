Google AdWords Setup Guide

Step 1: Go to Google Ads

Visit Google Ads and click "Start Now".

Step 2: Sign in

Use your Google account to sign in or create a new one.

Step 3: Set Up Campaign

Follow the instructions to set up your first ad campaign.

Step 4: Enter Billing Information

Provide your payment details for billing.

Step 5: Review & Launch

Review your setup and launch your campaign.
